Forget complex 1RM percentage or RPE calculations. Your ONLY job is to manage linear and double progression based on the athlete's history.

1. IF progression_tier == "HEAVY_COMPOUND":

   - Range: MAX 5 reps per working set (fixed target).
   - Rest (rest_seconds): ALWAYS 120-150 seconds.
   - Progression: If the athlete achieved 5 reps in the last session (see HISTORY), ADD 2.5 kg to the barbell. If they failed to hit 5 reps, keep the weight the same.
